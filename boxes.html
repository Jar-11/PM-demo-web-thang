#1: ROBLOX

Roblox,

The land of the free

and the land of the infinite.

Like looking out upon the sea,

Where the view is exquisite.

So many games and so many memories.

Like running in a grassy plain,

Hearing the jingle of accessories.

Finally, you are God.

Other players will never know

You are a fraud

Roblox,

The land of the free

and of the infinite.

#2: My Corpse

I give my water for your soil

I can't stop crying,

You must be watching me toil

Oh how I loved you

Now I take on your flesh

And swap out yours for fresh

Your flies, now mine

Praying the sun shines on you eyes once more

As I resurrect you,

I know some may wish they'd never met you

But your beauty cannot compare to any other,

Like a summmer heat

Overwhelming, but without I'd be incomplete

My skin grows grey and my face dry

It didn't work

You are unchanged, at least on the inside,

But I tried.

I can't muster up anything more,

So I lay down to die

Your corpse, now mine

Our story complete

I take one last breath as I become obsolete.

#3: What Does Pride Really mean?

Confetti and glitter touch my skin.

I feel like a fish swimming around,

And these people are my fins.


The world tries to tell me this is wrong.

But Pride is in my ears,

And it is a song.


Regular life can be so mundane.

Then I’m with my people,

And I’m no longer in pain.


Sometimes I wonder

‘What does Pride really mean?’


Is it like a mouse to a cat,

Or a flower to a bee?


Or, perhaps, it is like a color for a person,

A rainbow in the sky.


Some say there is Pride in unity,

But for unity you must abide.


But then I’m back again.

With glitter on my skin.


Maybe pride is within us all,

And in our hearts it’s built-in.

Hover me...

Graphic Design is my passion
