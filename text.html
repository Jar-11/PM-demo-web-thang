    • The best food ever made

    Made by C.J. Feckovic

    Tortellini is a small stuffed pasta from Italy. There are different kinds of stuffing for tortellini.

    Some of those are:

    1. Parmesan cheese
    2. Pork
    3. Beef
    4. Ricotta cheese
    5. Romano cheese
    6. Spinach
    Tortellini is fun to chew. Its texture and size is similar to ravioli but a little smaller.
